The source for all your Miami-Dade voter needs.

Rep Miami is a seamless way to connect constituents to their representatives. We provide names, links to personal websites, and phone numbers. Here are some possible reasons why this might be important:

  • Feedback about a certain government policy (local or otherwise)
  • Learning more about an incumbent candidate before elections
  • Sharing a way in which our government may improve

Rep Miami was created by Code For Miami, a volunteer techy organization. Find out about (or even join?) Code for Miami at our website here. We love meeting new people!
